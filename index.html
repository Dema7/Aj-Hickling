Aj Hickling

Piano busker

Queenstown, New Zealand

While walking in a chill day along the streets of Queenstown, we heard a pleasant music coming from nowhere, but after few steps we came across this guy.

He had a humble looking and he was shy when questioned about his music but when he concentrated on his piano he suddenly became a piano master. Lots of people stopped and listened to his music in awe.

Location:

Queenstown, Earl Street, lake view.
